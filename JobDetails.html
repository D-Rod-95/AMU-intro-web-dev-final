Job Description

At Nike, a Software Engineer II, EADP will be part of the Enterprise Architecture & Developer Platforms team, focusing on building cloud-native platforms that scale with the business demands. This role involves designing and developing tools to improve security, reliability, and performance while collaborating with various teams within Nike.

Responsibilities

  • Evangelize and adopt Global Platforms and agile principles
  • Design and develop scalable, resilient cloud-native architecture
  • Deliver well-documented and tested code
  • Collaborate with other development and architecture teams
  • Support product/service scoping activities

Required Skills

  • Strong understanding of technical concepts
  • Experience with Grafana Dashboards and cloud platforms
  • Proficiency in programming languages like Python, Java, Go
  • Excellent communication and data analysis skills
  • Experience in IT infrastructure monitoring and observability

Education and Training

A Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science or a related field is required. Additionally, at least 4 years of experience in large-scale production-grade software development and platform engineering is necessary.

Salary and Benefits

Nike offers a competitive salary along with a generous total rewards package, a casual work environment, and a diverse and inclusive culture. Employees are encouraged to grow professionally and personally while contributing to the company's mission to bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete in the world.
